Kathleen

Kathleen was busy finishing a Ph.D., teaching kids to read, and biking her tail off. So it's understandable that she would confuse the Match.com button for "Save For Later" with the "Tell Him He's Dreamy" button. And likewise, when she realized that mistake, and then sent this dreamy guy a "wink" thinking she was undoing the "Tell Him He's Dreamy." Because, c'mon, Internet dating is hard. Lucky for this guy, he didn't get in the way of the Ph.D. Or the biking. Her family knew it was serious when Reid made it past the record 3-month mark.

She said YES!

Reid

Most of his friends and family thought he'd lost his mind when he took a job in Lansing, Michigan. But Reid loved the news and wanted to bring his digital savvy to the business. He also used that digital savvy to comb Match.com for a rare creature: over-educated, over-active, and extra-tolerant of guys with wanderlust. Lucky Reid, he found his match. When his work took him to Detroit — even farther from Kathleen's Kalamazoo — he knew he had to snag this lady permanently. Reid asked Kathleen to marry him in the mountains of Costa Rica.
